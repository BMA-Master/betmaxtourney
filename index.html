How to Compete

Tournament mechanics explained

At the end of each tournament, standings are determined by highest TD$ (Tournament Dollars) balance:

1

Join a Free Tournament

Browse live tournaments across NFL, NBA, MLB and more. No entry fee, no credit card required.

2

Receive Your TD$ Budget

Everyone starts with equal TD$. How you allocate your budget is your strategy.

3

Make Picks & Compete

Pick spreads, totals, player props across all games. Watch your rank climb in real-time.

Strategic Depth: Assign confidence levels to your picks. Higher confidence = more TD$ at risk. Similar bet types to standard sportsbooks—spreads, totals, moneylines, and player props.
